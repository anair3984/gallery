I'm a woman, a daughter, a student, and I hate Sanjana

Join me as I discuss my life and backstory.

By Anushca Nair

Introduction

I was born on November 17th 2005 in Hyderabad India. I live in Concord NC with my mom, my dad, my older brother, and my dog .For the first two years of my life I lived in India with just my mom and brother. After the three of us moved to the United States of America to live with my dad.

Interests

In my free time I like to do everything. Typically I like to go out with friends, play volleyball, bake, cook, listen to music, dance, or workout. On a typical weekend I like to sleep in to make up for missing sleep from the week.

Chupa chups I love tiramisu dragée danish sweet sesame snaps marzipan shortbread. Gummies dessert candy canes caramels bear claw cupcake dessert. Chocolate bonbon croissant chocolate bar candy soufflé jelly. 

Extra

I also work for a photographer and in the job I get to manage all the events. Usually these events are parties, religious ceremonies, weddings. I coordinate everyone’s job and I can also host these events. I really enjoy my job because I get to work with my brother and one of my close friends.

Working was one of the things in life I was actually really excited for. I started off working for a family friend. We have known them for over 6 years and they come to our house at least once a week. I do want to apply for a job outside of this one but I am not sure exactly where. I know I do want to work in the food industry.
